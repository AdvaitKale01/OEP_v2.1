Paper Pattern

  1. The Exam is of limited time duration of 30 Minutes.
  2. There are 10 Questions in the test.
  3. Each Question is compulsory.
  4. For every correct answer, +3 Marks will be awarded and for every incorrect answer, -1 Marks will be added.
  5. The questions are of MCQ type in which you have to choose the best out of 4 options.
